Coming soon after more testing. It's summer in Texas and like 235203845109857 degrees in my garage so I don't currently train with the garage door closed (seriously it's an oven) and can't do some decent testing.

Until I make a way to account for lens distortion, you'll need to use a webcam with no fisheye effect. Also, the marker on your barbell must be a unique color. I use a 3" wooden disk spray painted fluorescent orange mounted to the end of a short piece of PVC that I put on the end of the barbell. This works as long as there is nothing larger than this disk that is the same color in the field of view!
